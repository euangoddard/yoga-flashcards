Glossary

English Sanskrit Meaning
Adho अधो downward
Ardha अर्ध half
Bandha बंध lock
Dhánus धनुः bow
Dvī द्वि two
Eka एक one, single
Parivṛtta परिवृत्त revolved, twisted
Supta सुप्त supine, reclining
Tulā तुला balance
Upaviṣṭha उपविष्ठ seated
Ubhaya उभय both, together
Ūrdhva ऊर्ध्व upward
Utthita उत्थित extended
